Jerseys, bouquets and promises...

Winner of his third Tour de France, Chris Froome wore the Yellow Jersey in controlling the competition beginning at the Bagnères-de-Luchon stage just until the Champs-Elysées. The Briton had to deal with new challengers from Adam Yates to Romain Bardet. The stage victories of Mark Cavendish, Peter Sagan and Tom Dumoulin contributed to the daily spectacle.
